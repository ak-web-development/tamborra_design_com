Welcome to Tamborra Design Group


I received my Bachelor of Architectural Degree from New York Institute of Technology, Old Westbury, Long Island in 1980. I subsequently established my own firm in 1987 and since 1980 I have been involved with over two hundred and fifty projects with an estimated construction value of over $750 Million Dollars.

My goal at Tamborra Architecture & Construction, P.C. is to design your project so as to fulfill all of your dreams and needs. From the early design stages to the day that you take occupancy, whether it is a new home or retail/office space, our team is there to assist you in any way possible. My associates are professional, courteous, and knowledgeable. If you can dream it, Tamborra Architecture & Construction, P.C. can make it a reality. Architectural State Licenses: New York, New Jersey, NCARB. Florida & Mass. Pending.
